Fighter Aircrafts

What are fighter aircrafts?

Fighter aircrafts are designed to engage in air-to-air combat and do so to secure or defend a specific part of an airspace. This means fighter jets play an essential part in war and a status of power, which is why so many goverments would rather put taxpayer's money into military assets such as these aircrafts than into free healthcare. (Not being specific.)

Strengths of fighter aircrafts

    • High maneuverability
      Heavy firepower
      Very. Very fast.

    • Weaknesses of fighter aircrafts

    • Expensive
      Hard to pilot;

      High maintence
