Stealth Aircrafts

What are stealth aircrafts?

A stealth aircraft is, well, stealthy. It is an aerial military technology intended to make the vehicle avoid detection. They do this by reducing the reflection/emission of radar, infared, radio frequency (RF) spectrum, and audio.

Strengths of bomber aircrafts

    • Avoids detection;
      Weapons are launched before enemies even know they exist;
      The element of surprise

    • Weaknesses of bomber aircrafts

    • No design is perfect and stealth technology may fail
