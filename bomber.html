Bomber Aircrafts

What are bomber aircrafts?

A bomber aircraft is a type of military aircraft designed to drop bombs on surface targets. The first ever record of such "aerial bombardment" can be traced back to the Italo-Turkish War, when an italian pilot dropped four grenades off his airplane on two Turkish targets.

Strengths of bomber aircrafts

    • Massive payload capacity
      Long-range
      Usage of nuclear weapon (really powerful)

    • Weaknesses of bomber aircrafts

    • Slow
      Reliance on support;
      Susceptible to attacks when alone
      High maintence

    • Famous bomber aircrafts:

    • Northrop B-2 Spirit
      Boeing B-52 Stratofortress
      Us B-17 Flying Fortgress
