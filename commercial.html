What are commerical aircrafts?

A commerical aircraft is a type of civil aircraft designed to carry passengers from one airport to another. The function is in its name, for commerical aircrafts is the foundation to aviation industry and generate profits by getting paid to fly people.

Strengths of commerical aircrafts

    • Efficent at transporting masses
      Connects countries; creating a transportation that is global
      Accessible to civilians
